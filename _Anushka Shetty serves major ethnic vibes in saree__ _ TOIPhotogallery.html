Actor by chance

Hailing from an ethnic Tulu family, Anushka never considered becoming an actress.

Image Source: Instagram-Anushka-Shetty

Teacher by choice

The actress used to teach children from the third standard before becoming a star.

Image Source: Instagram-Anushka-Shetty

Yoga trainer

After being trained by the spiritual teacher Bharat Thakur, she used to conduct yoga sessions in Mumbai.

Image Source: Instagram-Anushka-Shetty

Debut movie

Meher Ramesh spotted Anuska and recommended her to Puri Jagan for his upcoming movie ‘Super’ (2005), which starred Akkineni Nagarjuna. She was one of the two leading ladies in the movie and was nominated for the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actress - Telugu.

Image Source: Instagram-Anushka-Shetty

Ready to leave

After performing in 'Super' and 'Mahanandi', she was ready to return to her hometown, as those movies didn't do well at the box office.

Image Source: Instagram-Anushka-Shetty

Big break

Director S.S. Rajamouli changed her career as she got a major break with ‘Vikramarkudu’ (2006), which starred Ravi Teja.

Image Source: Instagram-Anushka-Shetty

Hit movies

Solo success

Anushka has enjoyed a bigger solo success with the horror movie ‘Arundhati’ (2009). The actress has been featured in over thirty movies.

Blockbuster actress
